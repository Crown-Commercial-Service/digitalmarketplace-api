Dear Seller,

We are writing to remind you that one or both of your insurance documents on the Digital Marketplace have expired or are about to expire. To remain eligible, we require that all sellers maintain updated documents.

Which documents does this apply to?

The documents we need you to check and update (as required) are your:

  • professional indemnity and public liability insurance
  • workers compensation insurance
These documents ensure buyers that your documentation is up to date, so they can source their digital needs with confidence.

What will happen if I don't update my documents

Sellers with expired documents will no longer be able to respond to opportunities on the Digital Marketplace.

How to update your documents:

  • log in to the Digital Marketplace
  • click dashboard > view your profile > update profile
  • open documents > upload your documents
  • click 'choose file' and upload your latest documents
  • click 'save and continue'

For help and guidance on updating documents
Please visit the financial and insurance documents page in the seller guide.

Questions or concerns
If you need further information or have any queries, please email us at marketplace@digital.gov.au

Thank you for your cooperation and your ongoing participation in the Marketplace. We look forward to reviewing your updated profile information soon.

Thanks,
The Digital Marketplace team
