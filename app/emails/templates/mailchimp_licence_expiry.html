Dear Seller,

We are writing to remind you that one of your labour hire licences on the Digital Marketplace have expired or are about to expire.

Some states operate under a mandated Labour Hire Licensing Act. You will require a relevant licence if you are applying for specialist opportunities in VIC, ACT or QLD.

How to update your licence details:

  1. From your Dashboard, select Update profile.
  2. Under Recruiter find the Labour hire licence section
  3. Update your licence details then select Save and continue
  4. Under Preview and submit, select Preview and submit updates
  5. Review your updates, and select Submit updates

Your update will be reviewed by our team.

Questions or concerns
If you need further information or have any queries, please email us at marketplace@dta.gov.au

Thank you for your cooperation and your ongoing participation in the Marketplace. We look forward to reviewing your updated profile information soon.

Thanks,
